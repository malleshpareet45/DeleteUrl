Account Deletion Request – ScanLash App

If you want to delete your account and all associated data from the ScanLash application, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account, click the button below to submit a deletion request:

Submit Account Deletion Request

You may also contact us directly at:

Email: support@scan-lash.com

Data Deleted After Account Removal

  • User profile (name, email, phone)
  • Authentication credentials
  • Uploaded images or media
  • In-app usage data linked to your identity

Data That May Be Retained

  • Transaction logs for legal or financial requirements
  • Non-identifiable analytics data

Data Retention Period

All personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of receiving your deletion request.
